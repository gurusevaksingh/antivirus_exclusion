Exclusion of Avast Antivirus

Way to exclude files and folder from Avast Antivirus are:

  • First of all open your Avast Antivirus security in your computer.
    • Click on Menu Button.
      • Click on Setting Button.
        • Click on Exception Button.
          • Click on Add Exception Button.
            • Click on Browse Button .
              • Enter the path of the file or folder in the block.
                • After select the path Click on Add Exception Button.
                  • Click Block and Allowed App Button.
                    • Click on Select App Manualy Button.
                      • Select the path of App.
