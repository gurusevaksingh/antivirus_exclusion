Exclusion of Kaspersky Antivirus

Way to exclude files and folder from Kaspersky antivirus are:

  • First of all open your Quick Heal antivirus security in your computer.
    • Click on Setting Button.
      • Click on Additional Button.
        • Click on Threat and Exclusions Button.
          • Click on Configure Exclusion Rules in Threat and Exclusions Button.
            • Click on add Button Exclusion Rules.
              • Enter the path of the file or folder in the block. and check all three points for exclusion. After all click add Button.
